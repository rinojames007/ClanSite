CLAN RULES

1. Strictly follow clan mail
- Warning on 1st war and kick if repeated on future wars.

2. Discord is mandatory for each and every member
- It will help us get contact you and vice versa ASAP.
- It will also help us out to keep a track of your participation in clan activities and channel inactive players out of the clan.

3. No more than 2 ids per player in the clan.

4. CWL :
- We play Lazy CWL.
- Tho, cwl is optional but if you're willing to participate in CWLs, then you're bound to play with us.
- Staying in CWL clans for too long will get you temporarily kicked from cwl clans.
- No begging for bonuses, we keep a track of the winners and award accordingly. So, play every season to increase your chance.

THE STRIKE SYSTEM

Purpose:
• To promote positive behavior and maintain a respectful and enjoyable environment.
• To keep inactivity out of the clan.

Starting Point:
• Every member will be awarded 5 points upon joining the clan.

Positive Points:

+1 points:
• 2k points in CG
• Donation ratio of 1:2 or above
• Not missing more than 4 wars in a season

+2 points:
• Active participation in clan raids the whole season
• Donation ration higher than 1:1
• Max points in CG
• Not missing a single war in a season

Negative Points:

-1 points:
• Doing wrong attacks in wars after an initial warning
• Not attacking in more than 3 wars continuously
• Not completing 1k points in clan games
• Not having at least a 1:4 donation ratio by the end of the season

Removal: A player will be removed from the clan if they have a total of 0 or fewer points.

Note: The strike system is subject to review and revision by the clan leaders to ensure its effectiveness and fairness.
